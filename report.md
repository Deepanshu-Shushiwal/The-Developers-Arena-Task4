# E-Commerce Sales Analysis Report

## Objective
To analyze e-commerce sales data and identify sales patterns using data visualization techniques.

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains sales records including product type, quantity sold, price, region, and total sales.

## Analysis Performed
- Calculated total sales
- Analyzed sales by product and region
- Studied sales trend over time

## Visualizations
- Bar chart compares sales across products
- Line chart shows sales trend over time
- Pie chart represents sales distribution by region

## Insights
- Laptops and Phones generate the highest revenue
- The North and East regions contribute most to total sales
- Sales show variation across dates indicating demand fluctuations

## Conclusion
This project demonstrates how data visualization helps in understanding business performance and supports decision-making.
